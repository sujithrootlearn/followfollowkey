!Follow.Follow.Mee!

STAGE:0 : SCORE:0 : MISS:0

Game Instructions:

  • This is a 3 stage game, 0 ,1 and 3 with 2 different levels, Beginner and Expert.
  • Goal is to hit max score of 30 with no key miss to finish the game.
  • Bubbles in red indicates that they are going to disappear so catch them.
  • Max score of each stage is 10 with no key miss to move forward.
  • Remember that if any key miss do not hit max score. Wait for 'Yellow' bubbles.
  • Use 'Yellow' bubbles to make miss count to 0.
  • Never miss any Yellow bubble and follow the red bubbles immediately.
